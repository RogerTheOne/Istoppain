Why Acupunture Work?

Acupuncture and other Chinese medicines, based on the body's meridian energy system discovered over 5,000 years ago, address ailments and emotional disorders by restoring energy balance. Stimulating acu-points with acupuncture promotes the body's self-healing and addresses the root cause of illnesses, leading to long-term healing.

Additionally, these traditional Chinese medicines are effective in preventing diseases by identifying and correcting imbalances before they become serious.

What Is Herbalism?

Chinese herbal medicine is highly sophisticated, with a vast repository of knowledge developed over millennia. This ancient practice includes countless administrations over thousands of years.

The Chinese herbal pharmacopoeia catalogs over 10,000 natural substances, referred to as "herbs," which include thousands of plant species from around the world, as well as mineral and animal materials.
